Ashmit Khandelwal

Research Fellow at Microsoft Research

background: Hallstätter See, Austria

I’m a researcher at Microsoft Research working with Dr. Nagarajan Natarajan and Dr. Amit Sharma on search and reasoning with Large Language Models (LLMs). I study how these systems can search, analyze, and verify information at scale, and methods to make their reasoning more reliable and controllable.

My current research focuses on Deep Research (DR): a paradigm where LLM systems perform structured search over large corpora. I’ve worked on giving a definition to and evaluating DR. I’m now focused on developing methods to verify these systems’ claims, and steering their search processes. Looking ahead, I aim to extend my research to more active domains of information, such as automated computer use and misinformation mitigation in social media.

Previously, I finished my Undergraduate at BITS Pilani, India where I studied Computer Science and Data Science. I spent a summer at the Adobe MDSR lab, where I worked with on predicting human behavior at scale using multimodal LLMs. This research was a spotlight publication at ICLR 2024.
